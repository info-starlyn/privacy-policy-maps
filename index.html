Privacy Policy

App: Map Compass  |  Developer: Starlyn Interactive  |  Last updated: March 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Map Compass collects, how it is used, and your choices. By using the app you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

a) Location

Map Compass requests access to your device's precise and approximate location (GPS). Location is used to:

  • Display your current position on the map.
  • Provide compass bearing relative to your position.
  • Autofill coordinates when saving a measurement or special location.

Your location is not stored on any server. It is used only on-device in real time and discarded when the app is closed. When you perform a location search, your search query (text only, not your GPS coordinates) is sent to OpenStreetMap Nominatim (see Section 3).

b) Measurements and Saved Locations

Bearing measurements and special locations you save are stored locally on your device using Android DataStore. This data includes coordinates (latitude/longitude), bearing values, timestamps, and any custom name you provide. It is never transmitted to our servers.

c) App Settings and Preferences

Your in-app preferences (theme, units, language, map style, etc.) are stored locally on your device and are not shared with us.

d) Usage Data

The app tracks how many seconds per day you use certain features and how many measurements or locations you add per day, solely to enforce free-tier usage limits. This data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any account information.
  • We do not collect crash reports or diagnostic data.
  • We do not use analytics services (e.g., Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel).
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, microphone, files, or calendar.
  • We do not build advertising profiles or track you across other apps.

3. Third-Party Services

Google Play Services

The app uses Google Play Services for:

  • Location (FusedLocationProvider) – Provides GPS data on-device. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Play Billing – Manages subscription purchases through the Google Play Store. Google processes payment data; we do not receive or store your payment information.
  • AdMob (Advertising) – If you are on the free tier, the app may show rewarded video ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use your Advertising ID and other device information to serve personalised or non-personalised ads in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalised ads in your device's Google Settings.

OpenStreetMap Nominatim

When you search for a place, your search text is sent to the OpenStreetMap Nominatim geocoding service (nominatim.openstreetmap.org). No account or personal information is included. Nominatim's use of this data is governed by the OpenStreetMap Foundation Privacy Policy.

OpenFreeMap / MapLibre

Map tiles are fetched from OpenFreeMap's tile servers (tiles.openfreemap.org). Your device's IP address and the map viewport you are viewing are inherently included in tile requests. See openfreemap.org for details.

4. Data Storage and Security

All app data (measurements, locations, preferences, usage counters) is stored locally on your device using Android's DataStore. It is never sent to our servers. Android's built-in backup service may sync this data to your Google account if you have device backup enabled; you can control this in your device's backup settings.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your locally stored data remains on your device until you delete it within the app or uninstall the app. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

6. Children's Privacy

Map Compass is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data through the app, please contact us so we can address it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
